Uninstall AutoReloadPro

Are you sure you want to uninstall AutoReloadPro?

Uninstalling AutoReloadPro will remove the extension from your browser. You will no longer be able to automatically refresh web pages at specified intervals.

If you're experiencing any issues or have feedback, we'd appreciate it if you could let us know before uninstalling.

If you'd like to keep AutoReloadPro but disable it temporarily, you can do so by disabling the extension in your browser's settings.

To uninstall, click the "Uninstall" button below. If you'd like to keep the extension, click "Cancel".
