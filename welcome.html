More Information

AutoReloadPro is a Chrome extension developed by sinjith, aimed at automating the process of refreshing web pages at specified intervals. Whether you're monitoring live updates, tracking auctions, or simply want to keep content up-to-date, AutoReloadPro saves you time by automatically refreshing the current tab without manual intervention.
